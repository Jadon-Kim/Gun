Sniper Rifle

Explanation about sniper rifles, June 23rd, 2022

Sniper rifle is a rifle with a high-precision, long-range.

Sniper rifles are designed and manufactured for use in killing human beings or prey at a huge range, and there is a telescopic sight attatched to the sniper rifle, leading to the extreme accuracy.

Sniper Rifle Types

Types of Sniper Rifles, June 23rd, 2022

Barrett M82, AWM

There are some examples of Sniper Rifles; Barret M82 is used as anti-material weapon and the strongest among other sniper rifles, and AWM is used for bolt action, and it is very deadly to use.
