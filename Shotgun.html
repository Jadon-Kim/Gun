Shotgun

Explanation about shotguns, June 22, 2022

Shotgun is a smoothbore shoulder weapon designed to fire a number of pellets that spread in a diverging pattern after they leave the muzzle.

Shotguns have long barrels and usually have smooth bores so that they could reduce their friction after people shoot with shotguns. They're usually used for flying targets.

Shotgun Types

Types of Shotgun, June 23rd, 2022

S12K, M590

There are examples of shotguns: S12K is good for range but has weak power, and M590 which is a 12-gauge shotgun and used to shoot a lot of multiple projectiles to the air.
