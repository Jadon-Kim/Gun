Pistol

Explanation about pistols, June 18th, 2022

Pistol is a small firearm designed for one-hand use.

Pistol is a handgun, which is small and normally lighter than other kind of gun. It is useful when multiple bullets should be quickly be fired, and it is the most popular option for both self defense and home defense.

Pistol Types

Types of pistols, June 18th, 2022

M1911, Glock19, BerettaM9

There are some examples of pistols: M1911, Glock19, BerettaM9. M1911 pistol is used for single action, Glock19 for double action, and BerettaM9 for triple action.
