Rifle

Explanation about rifles, June 21st, 2022

A rifle is a shoulder-fired long gun, with a series of spiral grooves cut inside the barrel ("rifling") imparting spin to the projectile.

Rifles are pretty heavy among the guns, and they have a great ammunition capacity. Also, they have a very good accuracy, so it is good to shoot people with rifles. However, 2 hands are needed to operate a single rifle.

Rifle Types

Types of Rifles, June 22nd, 2022

AK47, M4A1

There are some examples of rifles: AK47, which is pretty made of wood, and M4A1, which has semi-automatic and fully automatic firing mode.
